Craft Beer Berlin

By Jay \\ Apr 4 2016

I love all beer, but I have a special appreciation for the small-time craft-beer brewers putting money and effort into making a quality product for me to enjoy. Berlin's craft beer scene is blowing up, with a lot of players brewing in Berlin, and even more bar owners sourcing great beer from around the world for Berliners to enjoy.

I'm lucky enough to live near Bierlieb who have an absolutely outstanding range of craft beer as well as events going on pretty regularly. Find their Facebook page to stay updated.

I'm also lucky that a stone's throw in every direction there are a whole lot of other shops, restaurants and bars serving great beer that I love. I'll be updating this map when I find a new place that I think fits the bill - which is probably too regularly :D
